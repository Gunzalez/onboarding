About_

Menu

What and why

Brought to you by Chris, Tizzy, Segun and supporting cast Andrea.

Purpose

The initial intention was for a helpful tool to aid new joiners, to guide their way around Valtech, preferably mobile based.

Content

This prototype will help determine what content goes in, the target audience - just new joiners or everyone, data access levels, privacy, if we need a dedicated CMS and such and such.

Technology

Currently this is a web app, but we hope it helps you visualise and decide on whether to keep it as web app, or build an iOS version, or maybe scrap it all together due to lack of resources.

Feedback

This is just the very start and we need your input to make it work for everyone. Suggestions, questions, LOLs, flowers, please send them all to tizzy.jones@valtech.co.uk.
